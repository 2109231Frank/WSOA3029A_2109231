ABOUT

This website explores the quality of living in different places on the planet. The idea behind this is to confront people with the fact that there are many areas on the globe where people struggle through life and there are other places where people are born into luxury and safety. Why do I think this is important? Well, I have spoken to numerous people who are overly pessimistic or optimistic about the place that they live. I personally become tangled in my own pessimism sometimes and think that it would be beneficial to have a bit more perspective with regards to quality of living and the privilege that I actually have.
