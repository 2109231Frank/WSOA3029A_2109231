STYLE GUIDE

This website is focused on data visualization. I wanted to keep things simple seeing that simple websites are easier to digest in my opinion and I like the look of clean websites. I decided to use some of my favourite colours seeing that they complement each other well while having good contrasts with one another. My main goal for this website is to focus on the graphs rather than making the website pretty. That being said, I will try to make the website look nice.

RATIONALE

The reason I decided on using the data that I did is because I wanted to share perspective on Johannesburg and which aspects of the city needs to be considered with regards to quality of life. I feel that a lot of people (myself included) get lost in pessimism or optimism when talking about how much we love or hate Johannesburg. I wanted to make a graph that shows which aspects of the city are good and which aspects need to be kept in mind while living here.
